What I use

Equipment and Software

Midi Keyboard

I use the M-Audio keystation-88es. I got it years ago and I really like it. Funnily enough, the keys yellowed in the sun while it sat in my window for many years, making it have essentially a gradient when it comes to key color. Oh well!

DAW

Currently I am using reaper, but in the past I have used Tracktion Waveform, which was also good.

Plugins

I mainly use Surge Synth, Spitfire Labs/BBC Orchestra, and Kontakt Player. If you notice a theme of free software (as in cost, although I appreciate FOSS as well), you'd be right. I like using free plugins. For effects I really just use Reaper's built-in plugins.
